Welcome to EduGuideBot!

This is a companion website for the EduGuideBot Telegram bot that helps Cambodian students find the right university for their educational journey.

Start the Bot on Telegram

How it works

1. Chat with the Bot

Start a conversation with EduGuideBot on Telegram and answer a few simple questions about your preferences.

2. Get Personalized Recommendations

The bot will analyze your answers and suggest universities that best match your profile.

3. View Detailed Results

Access this website to view detailed information about your recommended universities.

About EduGuideBot

EduGuideBot was created to help Cambodian high school graduates make informed decisions about their higher education options. The bot provides personalized university recommendations based on various factors including location preference, budget, academic interests, and career goals.

Our database includes detailed information about universities across Cambodia, with a focus on programs, tuition fees, facilities, and other factors that are important for students when choosing a university.
